The heritage of Kolkata

A city best seen from the right seat.

Kolkata isn't a place you tick off in an afternoon. It's the rattle of a tram down Esplanade, the ironwork of the Howrah Bridge at dusk, the marble hush of the Victoria Memorial, and clay gods taking shape in the lanes of Kumartuli.

After nearly thirty years driving these streets, we don't just take you to the landmarks — we know when the light is good at Prinsep Ghat, which by-lane skips the College Street jam, and where to stop for the city's best kathi roll.

  • Victoria Memorial & the Maidan
  • Howrah Bridge & Mullick Ghat flower market
  • Kumartuli idol-makers' quarter
  • College Street & the Coffee House
  • Dakshineswar & Belur Math
  • Prinsep Ghat & the riverfront trams

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Which areas do you cover?
We cover all of Kolkata and the airport, plus outstation trips across West Bengal, Sikkim, Odisha and eastern India — from a short city drop to a multi-day tour.
How do I book a car?
Call or WhatsApp 98042 49776, or fill the enquiry form on this page. Abhijit confirms the vehicle, route and fare with you directly before the trip.
What vehicles are available?
Air-conditioned sedans (Dzire or Etios) for up to 4 passengers, and 12 to 17 seat tempo travellers for larger groups. Every vehicle comes with an experienced driver.
Do you do airport pickups and drops?
Yes — pickups and drops at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport and anywhere in the city, including early-morning and late-night flights.
How is the fare calculated for outstation trips?
Outstation fares depend on the distance, the number of days and the vehicle. You get a clear, all-in quote before the journey, so there are no surprises at the end.
Can you plan a full group tour?
Yes. Tell us the destination and dates and we arrange the vehicle, route, stops and timing — for families, office outings or pilgrim groups.
